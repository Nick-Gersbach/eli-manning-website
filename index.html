Eli Manning

Eli Manning is the current starting quarterback for the New York football Giants. He is a two time super bowl MVP and he is famous for beating the previously undefeated New England Patriots in super bowl XLII. Eli is nearing the end of his career, but with good protection and a running game, he is still be a force to reckoned with.
